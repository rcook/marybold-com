Computer Use by Seniors

The statistics are impressive: 30% of America's seniors between 55 and 75 are computer users.

The predictions are startling: by the year 2000, 60% of seniors will not only be on-computer, but on-line, as well.

In a large study funded by Intel in 1995, SeniorNet made a comprehensive study into the computing habits of seniors. The report summary is available on the Internet at the following URL:

http://www.seniornet.org/intute/survey2.html
