Fathering has emerged as a new issue in parenting, most identified with younger men who express a commitment to sharing in all aspects of parenting their children. Prominent in family field literature since the early 1990s, fathering has become a regular topic in the popular press, as well. Almost always, fathering is addressed in terms of men who live with their children, not in terms of non-residential or non-custodial parenting. (See also good dad/bad dad.)
