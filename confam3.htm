Nuclear family has most frequently referred to the biological unit of mother-father-children. In recent decades, the term has been used to refer to biological or adopted children. Some variations: traditional family, the standard package, normal configuration, ideal structure.

The “Ozzie and Harriet” model of nuclear family has been in place since the 1950s. Its definition would be full-time-employed Dad, at-home Mom, all children born after Dad and Mom’s marriage (first and only marriage for both of them.).
