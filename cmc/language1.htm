The Language of E-mail

Colloquialisms:

BCNU J/K
BTW PAW
FWIW PLS
GG ROFL
k RTFM
IMHO TTFN
IMNSHO YMMV

Self-Presentation:  The use of words or sayings in usernames (Deity, princess, cool guy, batman, etc.) or servers (@hotmail.com, @chickmail.com, @yahoo,com, etc) to create an image.

 

Punctuation:

capitalization to express emotion I AM SO MAD!
underscoring to indicate titles _Gone with the Wind_
asterisks something new *I got a job!
mock ellipsis denotes a pause I will get it done...sometime.
string of ellipses denotes a long pause .........I had to answer the phone
hyphens in words for emphasis I am really e-x-c-i-t-e-d!
strings of punctuation for emphasis ! ! ! ! ! or ? ? ? ? ?
