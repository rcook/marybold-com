Current Research Regarding Families and Technology

There is not much research on families and CMC to date, but what is available…

  • has often failed to meet informed consent required in other areas of research.

  • is subject to self-presentation by the user and therefore results are questionable. 

  • has been conducted with college students and with people at work, not in families.

To adequately study CMC in families, family scientists should…

  • meet informed consent standards. 

  • consider each family member individually.

  • also study dyads, extended family, and the family unit as a whole.

Researchers can choose from a variety of theoretical approaches including:

  • Social Presence Theory, Media Richness Theory, SIDE theory, Theory of Electronic Propinquity

  • Social Informational Processing Theory, Hyperpersonal Communication Framework

  • "Cues filtered out" theory, Uncertainty ReductionTheory

  • Cognitive Psychology, Information Processing

  • Family Development Theory

  • Systems Theory
