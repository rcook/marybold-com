The Impact of CMC on Intrafamilial Communications

Every generation has had its own concerns about advancing technology.  

Dime novels … Comic books … Radio … Television

Negative Impacts of CMC

  • May be used as a substitute for interaction

  • May lead to isolation

  • May lead to decreased communication between family members in the same home

Positive Impacts of CMC

  • Can increase communication between distant family members

  • May facilitate shared learning between generations

  • Closes the gap between parents and grown children

  • Can increase father/child communication

Convenient

Can be done at anytime
Can be one-on-one

Can be kept private or shared

Can be short without seeming abrupt

Can be constructed and edited at a leisurely pace

Makes communication easier for those with hearing loss

 May replace mom as gatekeeper of family communication
