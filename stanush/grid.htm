 Topic Grid

Diversity — the range of family composition from one adult + one child to multiple adults + multiple children. For curriculum purposes, the term family is therefore based on the assumption that a child is being parented by one or more adults.

Dynamic — the style of family relations, or pattern of relationships within the family system. For curriculum purposes, the range of family dynamic will focus on cooperative to anxiety-filled. Also, the topic will include information about child development.

Communication — the styles or patterns of information transfer among a group. For curriculum purposes, family communication will be demonstrated as being as diverse as families themselves.

Abuse — physical, emotional, and sexual behaviors that harm children. For curriculum purposes, the topic will include information about child development and realistic expectations.

Resilience — the general term for humans' capacity to adapt to or escape from adverse environments and better their own conditions, sometimes with help and sometimes with no help.
