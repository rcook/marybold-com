Illicit drugs in the previous 30 days

  • In 1998, 26% of 12th-graders reported using illicit drugs in the previous 30 days, as did 22% of 10th-graders and 12% of 8th-graders. Percentages increased substantially between 1992 and 1996.  Since 1996, rates have remained stable or have decreased. Long-term trends for seniors indicate that illicit drug use declined from 37 percent in 1980. (Data for 8th- and 10th-graders are not available before 1991.)

    1992 1996 1998
    8th grade 7% 15% 12%
    10th grade 11% 23% 22%
    12th grade 14% 25% 26%

  • Among 12th-graders, males are more likely to use illicit drugs than females. 

    1998 males females
    8th grade 12% 12%
    12th grade 29% 22%

  • Some differences are seen between ethnic groups.
