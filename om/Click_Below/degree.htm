Online Master's in Family Studies
at Texas Woman's University
    

Degree of 33 credit hours
  
11 courses comprise the Online Master's
All are available 100% online
On-campus courses are also available
   

Electronic Portfolio
  
The graduate writing requirement for the M.S. can be a thesis or portfolio. Typically, OM students select the portfolio, which reflects the course work of the Master's as well as the student's professional goals and philosophy. The portfolio is submitted in the final semester of courses and each student presents the work at a Seminar Week (either in person or through submission of a videotape or digital presentation).
   
  Professional Application 
 
Many assignments and readings in the Master's courses are geared to practical application, with opportunity for working professionals to create materials that can be put to use immediately.
