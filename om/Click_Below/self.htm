Flexible Course Load
  
 Full-time status in graduate school is 9 hours
Most of our students take 6 hours a semester
And a load of just 3 hours (1 course) is sometimes smart!
   

Course Rotation
  
You can start the Master's in any semester and adjust your course load to fit your schedule. At least two online courses are offered every semester. Some of the courses have regular spots on the Department rotation. For example, Research Methods is online every spring, Statistics is online every summer, and Lifespan Human Development is online every other semester.
   
  The Online Environment 
 
Some of the plusses of online education:
Any time, anywhere access to the course
Asynchronous discussion - for many different schedules
Advance assignments - work on the days best for you
Full participation - by everyone in the class
Shared documents - a chance to read others' projects
