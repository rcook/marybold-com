Online Master's in Family Studies
at Texas Woman's University
    

Admissions Requirements
  
GPA of 3.0 in last 60 hours of Bachelor's degree
No GRE required
Application through the TWU Graduate School
   

Identify Yourself as "Online"
  
On application, write "Online" alongside "Family Studies"
(Off-campus students receive support from several offices)
   
  How Long Does It Take? 
 
6 - 8 weeks, depending on arrival of transcripts
Order a transcript from every college or university ever attended
If there's a delay, register as "Non-degree seeking" for one semester
