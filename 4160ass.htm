Go to the syllabus for a brief description of assignments.

Due dates — In FLE, one of our tasks is to set the due dates for assignments as well as establish criteria for evaluation. Ideally, we will accomplish this during the first two class meetings. Then, a calendar of due dates will be posted at this URL.

Friendly warning — Sometimes a class unaccustomed to evaluation technique err and set standards that result in low grades for most of the class. This class may want to build in an “escape clause” and indicate that revisions may be made according to results from formative evaluation at mid-semester.
