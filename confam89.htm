Daily cigarette smoking  in the previous 30 days

  • In 1998, 9% of 8th-graders, 16% of 10th-graders, and 22% of 12th-graders reported smoking cigarettes daily in the previous 30 days.

  • Females and males report similar rates of daily smoking.  

    males females
    8th grade 8% 9%
    10th grade 15% 17%
    12th grade 23% 22%

  • Rates of smoking differ between ethnic groups. White students have the highest rate, followed by Hispanics, and then Blacks. In 1998, 28% of White 12th-graders reported daily smoking, compared to 14% of Hispanics and 7% of Blacks.
