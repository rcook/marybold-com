ePortfolio through TaskStream
adding attachments

Attachments can be added to any program (DRF, presentation folio, web folio, etc.) and can be in the form of virtually any file type. If you are submitting an exit portfolio for the M.S., be reminded that evaluators request PDF files (the sort read by Adobe Reader).

Step 1. Open an editing window by clicking on on the right-hand side of the screen:

Step 2. By default, the window opens to the space to enter text (the tab is labeled Text), but you can click on the tab called Attachments for this dialog box:

Step 3. Now, for the subtle nuances... the listing style of the attachments on your DRF or web page will depend on what is selected through the Preferences button (go back up to Step 1 to note where that yellow button is located). There's nothing intuitive about the words on the Preferences dialog box, but with a little guidance from our Resource How-To and some practice, the preferences will begin to make sense.
