Virus Safety Tips

Don’t recognize an email sender? Don’t open the message. Just select “Delete.” Your program may ask if you are sure, if you still want to throw it away without reading it. Confirm that. 

Sending to someone who may be as cautions as you? Use the subject line to announce your name and topic.

Ask your children to type their names in the subject line if they need to email you from an unknown (to you) email address.
