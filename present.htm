Care Fair at Lewisville I.S.D. in October 1999

Mary Bold and Mani, a favorite co-speaker

A frequent topic for Mani and me is anxiety, symbolized by “hot potatoes.” 

At the Care Fair we discussed anxiety in terms of intergenerational living and the sharing of holidays. Participants agreed that the transmission of anxiety within a group brings new meaning to the phrase “Pass the potatoes” at family dinners.
