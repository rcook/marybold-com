Five or more alcoholic beverages in a row in the last 2 weeks
(heavy drinking)

  • In 1998, heavy drinking remained stable from 1997, with figures of 32% for 12th-graders, 24% for 10th-graders, and 14% for 8th-graders.

  • Long-term trends for seniors indicate a peak in 1981 when 41% reported heavy drinking, and a low of 28% in 1993. 

  • As adolescents get older, the differences between males and females in this drinking behavior become more pronounced. 

    1998 males females
    8th grade 14% 13%
    10th grade 27% 22%
    12th grade 39% 24%

  • Heavy drinking appears to be much more likely among Hispanic and White students than among Black students.

    White Hispanic Black
    10th grade 27% 26% 13%
    12th grade 36% 28% 12%
